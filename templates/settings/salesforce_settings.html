Setup Instructions
  1. Log into your Salesforce org
  2. Go to Setup → Users → Users
  3. Click on your user profile
  4. Click "Reset My Security Token" to get your security token
  5. Check your email for the security token
  6. For subdomain, use the part before .salesforce.com in your org URL
Example:
If your Salesforce URL is mycompany.salesforce.com, your subdomain is mycompany
